Welcome To GOCLEAN

Keep our Environment Healthy

GoClean is Nigeria’s foremost waste recycling and social benefit venture that motivates post consumers to recycle by creating value from their everyday waste. We have developed an incentive based scheme, which involves collecting recyclable plastic bottles from post consumers, and in turn rewards them with “Points” which they can accumulate and use to redeem household items offered through our GoClean store or cash. We operate on a drop off and door to door collection of recyclable plastic bottles.

INNOVATION DRIVEN

GoClean Recycling seeks to innovatively address the plastic waste pollution in the West Africa region through a franchise model that integrates Waste pickers into the digital platform that enables homes, institutions and communities start and request recycling services with click of the finger.
